To read the rules
<highlight><tab><symbol>rules<end>

To read the changed rules
<highlight><tab><symbol>rules changes<end>

To read a specifc rule
<highlight><tab><symbol>rules 'id'<end>

To search for rules
<highlight><tab><symbol>rules search 'words'<end>

Note: The link to sign the rules is at the bottom of the rules.

<header2>Examples:<end>

Shows rule #2
<highlight><tab><symbol>rules 2<end>

Search for rules with the words 'pvp' or 'player'
<highlight><tab><symbol>rules search pvp player<end>
